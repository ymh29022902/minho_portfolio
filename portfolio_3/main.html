ROCKIN THE RIVERS LINE-UP

Known as “Montana's Home Grown Rockfest”

This festival has a reputation for bringing together fresh lineups and a rejuvenated spirit that plays hard off of our past history. It serves as the perfect excuse for people to pack up for the weekend, forget work and the rest of the world, and simply get out and have some fun here at one of America’s premier rock festival.

SEE LINE-UP

Many people come back year after year for this festival not only because of the great music and camaraderie of the other patrons but because of the extraordinary spirit they claim they feel here in this part of the country.

When a lot of like-minded people get together to just genuinely enjoy something as powerful as music in such a breathtakingly beautiful area like this, it creates a powerful energy and bond that can’t be found anywhere else.

Everyone becomes your friend, and you feel genuinely at home, even with the large and loud crowd surrounding you. Bonds have been made. Lifelong friendships have been formed. It’s an experience unlike any other.
