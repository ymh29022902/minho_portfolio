Festival With Water!

May 31, 2021 - Michael Dennis

● ● ●

TAGS : Entertainment Season

Montana is known for its beautiful rock scenery, but the Guadalupe River is also a waste to pass by. After thinking about how to enjoy this beautiful river, Rockin' the Rivers prepared a lot so that you can enjoy the water festival. During the Summer Festival, you can enjoy rock performances on tubes. In addition, those who own tickets to the Summer Festival can receive discounts at nearby accommodations and other amenities. In particular, Gruene and Huaco Raft lend kayaks to those who hold festival tickets at half the price, and if the person holding the festival ticket is staying here, they provide free service. In addition, there are various things to enjoy, such as salmon fishing and team competitions. Rockin' the Rivers shows us that there are not only rock performances that soothe the heat of summer, but also various water activities.

How to use Montana's Good Facility

May 29, 2021 - Sarah Michelle

● ● ●

TAGS : Camping Camping Facility Entertainment Season

Rockin' the Rivers has partnered with nearby companies to help those who visit the performance have more fun. This is a relationship where visitors can benefit from increasing their satisfaction by using accommodations or restaurants at a lower price, and nearby companies can benefit from increasing income as the number of tourists increases. Thank you for the measures to make rock lovers love Rockin' the Rivers, but you need to figure out which facilities provide a more comfortable and pleasant environment. In addition, most of the companies provide discount services to customers with festival tickets, of which some provide services at lower prices, so those who visit rock performances on couples or family trips will help finance. There is also a lottery event for performance visitors, and various prizes are available, ranging from free accommodation vouchers to free kayaking rental services and restaurant discount coupons.
